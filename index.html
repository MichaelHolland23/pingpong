Ping Pong!

Here's how it works:

Enter your number and Ping Pong will count to your number, listing all numbers in between. If your number is:

  • divisible by 3 you will 'ping'
  • divisible by 5 you will 'pong'
  • divisible by 15 you will 'ping-pong'
